:: Key ::
Cells are defined as groups of roads in a block shape a cell might look like "cell4_3NE" or "-cell1_2S"

"N E S W NE NW SE SW" is the orientation of the road in the cell.

"-" is used to denote a lane that travels westbound in the case of non-vertical lanes
     or southbound in the case of vertical lanes.
